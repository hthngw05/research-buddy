Core Capabilities

Everything you need to go from "confused student" to "research proposal".

PICO-Guided Refinement

Struggling to focus? The bot uses the PICO framework (Population, Intervention, Comparison, Outcome) to turn vague ideas into sharp clinical questions.

Expert Guidance PDFs

Access curated health science PDFs instantly. Get explanations on study designs, ethics, and methodology without leaving the chat.

Project Tracking

Save multiple projects to your personal database. List, edit, or delete them anytime. Your progress is saved for your next session.

PubMed Integration

Don't just plan—verify. The bot retrieves relevant PubMed articles based on your PICO inputs to ensure your topic has literature.

How it Works

A simple, structured workflow designed for research methodology.

01

Login & Start

Sign in to unlock the assistant. It creates a secure "Locker" for your work.

02

PICO Structure

Converse with the bot. It guides you to define Population, Intervention, Comparison, and Outcome.

03

Save & Source

Save your question. The bot then pulls initial PubMed articles to get you started.
